Welcome to CareConnect!


For all your health needs!

CareConnect is a web application that allows patients to connect with doctors, search for appointments, and book appointments. It also allows doctors to manage their appointments and patients. It is a one-stop solution for all your health needs. It also allows you to search for insurance providers and select insurance plans.

Patients

  • Search for Doctors
  • Book Appointments
  • Search for Insurance Providers
  • Select Insurance Providers
  • Leave Reviews for Doctors and Insurance Providers

Doctors

  • Advertise appointments
  • Get patient bookings
  • Patient Information beforehand (when shared)
  • Chat with other doctors
  • Sync with Insurance Providers

Insurance Providers

  • Advertise new plans
  • Conduct marketing
  • Get patient details for current plans
  • Sync with doctors
  • Get reviews for packages
